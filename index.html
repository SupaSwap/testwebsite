KIT PROTOCOL

KIT employs 3 simple functions: Reflection + LP acquisition + Burn In each trade, the transaction is taxed a 10% fee, which is split 2 ways. 5% fee = redistributed to all existing holders 5% fee is split 50/50 half of which is sold by the contract into BNB, while the other half of the KIT tokens are paired automatically with the previously mentioned BNB and added as a liquidity pair on Pancake Swap.
